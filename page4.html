Getting to Know Shkodra

General Information

Shkodra is a well-known city located in Northern Albania. It is mostly considered to be a relaxation destination for Albanians and a preferred place to enjoy the beauty of nature for tourists. The visitors to this city seem to enjoy its climate, the welcoming people, and the delicious food that the restaurants of the city offers. An interesting fact about this city is that almost everyone who lives here owns a bicycle and people choose to ride those rather than drive their cars.
