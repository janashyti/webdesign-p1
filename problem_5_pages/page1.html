Getting to Know Berat

General Information

Berat is a small town located in central Southern Albania. Berat is an infamous touristic attraction protected by UNESCO. This town is now more than 2400 years old and is especially liked for the traditional old houses that to this day continue to host tourists and visitors. Moreover, Berat is one of the only locations in the Balkan Penisula where people continue to live to live. The traditional cuisine of this town is also much preferred by all visitors.
