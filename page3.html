Getting to Know Tirana

General Information

Tirana is the capital city of Albania and therefore the biggest city in the country as well. More than half of the population of Albania currently lives in this city. That explains the constantly busy streets and the liveliness of the city. Like all other capitals worldwide Tirana is probably the most urbanized and industrialized city in the country with plenty of shopping malls, museums, and national monuments to visit. Tirana is also one of the cities that has the most coffee shops per Capita. If you do choose to visit Albania a night spent in Tirana would be a well-planned itinerary.
