Getting to Know Albania

General Information

Albania is a small country located in the southeast of Europe in the Balkan peninsula. It has a population of approximately 3 million people, however, there are a lot more Albanians living out of the country all around the world. Albania is well-known for its tourism and the beautiful sceneries that some of the cities offer. Another popular aspect Albania is known for is the traditional food that it offers. The traditional dishes have a wide variety and are well-liked among the natives and visitors.
